Project Description

Santa's Magic Night is a light-hearted Christmas-themed puzzle game for people of all ages.

Help Santa deliver presents to nice children in this cute Christmas puzzle game! Santa has just one night to travel all around the world and deliver presents to all the children on his nice list (naughty children don't get any presents).

Help Santa by guiding him to deliver presents. Power Santa's sleigh with Gingerbread Cookies and Christmas Magic. Pickup additional presents along the way to bring even more happiness.

This is a cute, accessible, and fun Christmas puzzle game, aimed for kids of all ages!

Studio

Enigma Dragons is an independent game studio established in 2017 by the brothers Silas Reinagel and Noah Reinagel.

The studio's goal is simple: to create enjoyable cerebral games that feel immensely satisfying to play and master.
