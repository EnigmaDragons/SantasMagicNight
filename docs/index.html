Cute Christmas puzzle game for fun-loving people of all ages.

Help Santa deliver presents to nice children in this cute Christmas puzzle game!

Santa has just one night to travel all around the world and deliver presents to all the children on his nice list (naughty children don't get any presents).

Help Santa by guiding him to deliver presents. Power Santa's sleigh with Gingerbread Cookies and Christmas Magic. Pickup additional presents along the way to bring even more happiness.

This is a cute, accessible, and fun Christmas puzzle game, aimed for kids of all ages!
