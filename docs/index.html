Cute Christmas puzzle game for fun-loving people of all ages.

The rules are simple, but the puzzles aren't.

BitVault is a logic puzzle game with a light-hearted Cyberpunk story of hacking, espionage, and epic corporate manipulation, for fans of The Witness and Sudoku.

BitVault follows in the footsteps of venerated puzzle genre greats like The Witness, Monument Valley, and Baba Is You.

​

  • ● Experience a fun story of modern hacking as a novice hacker.

  • ● Identify patterns where others just see chaos and colors.

  • ● Become an expert hacker by using your raw brainpower.

  • ● Solve over 60 challenging puzzles.
